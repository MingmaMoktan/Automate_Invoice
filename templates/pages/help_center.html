How accurate is the AI extraction?

Our neural engine typically achieves 98-99% accuracy on standard printed invoices. Handwritten notes or extremely blurry scans may require manual review.

What file formats are supported?

We currently support PDF, PNG, and JPG files. For best results, we recommend high-resolution PDF exports from your billing software.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All uploads are encrypted at rest and in transit. We use multi-tenant isolation to ensure your data is never accessible by other users.
